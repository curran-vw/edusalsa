Contact Maya

How to reach Maya Foster. Email is the best way to send a note about a review, a typo, or a tool update.

Send me a note

Email me at hello@edusalsa.com. I read every note. I will try to reply within two business days.

What to send

If you have a question about a tool, tell me your shop size and your goal. The more I know, the better I can help.

If you found a bug or a typo on the site, share the page link and the line you spotted. I will fix it fast.

If you run a tool that we cover and want to share an update, share the page link, what changed, and a source. I will look at it before our next yearly update.

What to skip

No mass cold pitches. No paid posts. No "guest blog" trades. I do not run those.

One more thing

I do not have a phone line. Email is the best way to reach me. It also gives me a chance to think before I reply.
