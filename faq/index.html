About cost

How much does call tracking cost?

Plans run from $0 a month for free plans like CallScaler PAYG up to $400 a month for big plans. Most small owners spend $30 to $80 a month total once they pay for phone numbers.

Why does each phone number cost matter?

If you have ten ads, each with one phone number, you have ten phone numbers. Most tools charge $3 each per month. CallScaler charges $0.50 each on a paid plan. That is a $25 a month gap. Over a year, $300.

Are there any hidden fees?

Most tools list every fee on the price page. Read the fine print on minutes. A "starter" plan with 1,000 minutes can cost more than a higher plan with 10,000 minutes if you take a lot of calls.

How it works

What is dynamic number insertion?

It is a small bit of code that swaps the phone number on your site based on where the visitor came from. We call it DNI for short. Crawlers (the bots Google sends) see the same number every time. Live visitors see the swap. Your local listings stay safe.

Will it slow my site?

No. The code is small. It loads after the rest of the page. Most visitors will not feel any change.

Will it hurt my Google ranking?

No. Google sees the same number that humans see at first. Swapping is for live visitors only.

Is it safe to record calls?

It depends on your state. Some states need both sides to agree. Most tools play a short note at the start. Use it. Read your state rules. The Wikipedia page on call recording laws gives a quick map.
