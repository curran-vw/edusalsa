About Maya Foster

Maya Foster is a teacher and trainer for small business owners. She runs edusalsa.com to help owners pick simple tools without big words.

About Maya Foster

Hi, I'm Maya. I teach small business owners how to use simple tools to grow. I have done this for ten years.

I started out as a coach for new freelancers. Most of them had the same problem: they ran ads and had no idea what worked. So I learned call tracking. I tested every tool I could find. I kept notes.

This site is the result of those notes. It is for owners like the ones I coach: smart adults who do not have time for big words and long talks.

I run my own small training shop on the side. I take a few one-on-one clients each year. The rest of my time goes to writing this site, making short videos, and answering owner questions by email.

What I believe

I believe most software is too hard for the people who need it. I think small business owners deserve clear, plain tools and clear, plain words. So that is what I write.

I will not push a tool I do not like. I will not hide a fee or a flaw. If a tool is not right for you, I will say so.

How I make money

This site earns a small fee when you sign up for a tool through one of my links. You pay nothing extra. I would write the same words even with no fee. That is why every review has both the good and the bad in it.

The full plan is on the How We Test page.

Get in touch

Send me a note from the contact page. I read every email. I cannot reply to all of them, but I try.
