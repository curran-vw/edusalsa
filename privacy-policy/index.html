Privacy Policy

What edusalsa.com collects when you visit. What we do not collect. How we use cookies. Your rights.

Last updated May 1, 2026.

This page tells you what info we collect when you visit edusalsa.com.

What we collect

  • Basic web logs (IP, browser type, the page that sent you here). Kept for up to 30 days.
  • Email replies, if you write to us. Kept as needed to help you.
  • Privacy-friendly site stats from a tool that does not use cross-site cookies.

What we do not collect

No ad trackers. No Facebook Pixel. No Google Ads remarketing. We do not sell data.

Cookies

We set no marketing cookies. Any cookie used is needed for the site to work.

Affiliate links

When you click an affiliate link, the tool may set a cookie to credit our site for the sign-up. We do not get your account info from the tool.

Your rights

If you live in the EU, UK, or California, you can ask us what data we have on you. You can ask us to delete it. Email us and we will reply within 30 days.

Children

This site is not for kids under 13.

Changes

If we change this page in a big way, we will note it at the top.
